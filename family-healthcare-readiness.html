The 2026 American Family Healthcare Readiness Survey™

Understanding the healthcare challenges facing American families.

Healthcare is no longer just about insurance. It is about access, affordability, prescription costs, mental wellness, and knowing your family has options when care is needed.

Why Your Voice Matters

Families are facing real healthcare pressure.

Rising medical expenses, longer wait times, prescription costs, and limited awareness of available resources are creating new challenges for households across the country.

What We Are Learning

Six areas that impact family healthcare readiness.

01

Healthcare Affordability

Understanding how rising medical costs affect household budgets, delayed care, and financial decisions.

02

Access to Care

Learning how quickly families can reach a physician when care is needed, including after hours.

03

Prescription Costs

Identifying whether medication expenses are causing families to skip, delay, or seek cheaper options.

04

Employer Benefits

Helping families understand current benefits, including whether an Employee Assistance Program is available.

05

Mental Wellness

Measuring awareness of counseling and emotional wellness resources before a crisis occurs.

06

Family Priorities

Discovering which healthcare resources families value most: convenience, affordability, prescriptions, or access.
